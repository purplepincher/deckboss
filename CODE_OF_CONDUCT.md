# Code of Conduct

This project's contributors include working fishermen who may not be
developers, and developers who've never set foot on a commercial boat.
Both are welcome, and neither should be made to feel like they don't
belong here.

## The short version

Be respectful. Disagree about code, architecture, or domain facts as
directly as you want — that's normal and healthy. Don't be dismissive of
someone's contribution because of how they wrote an issue, what they know
or don't know about software, or what they know or don't know about
fishing. A one-line bug report from someone using the app on a real deck
is worth exactly as much respect as a well-formatted pull request.

Not acceptable, anywhere in this project's spaces (issues, PRs,
discussions): harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language or
jokes, or publishing someone else's private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies to this repository (issues, pull requests, discussions) and
any other official PurplePincher project spaces.

## Enforcement

This is a small, single-maintainer project — enforcement means the
maintainer will remove content, close threads, or block participants who
violate this. Report a concern by opening an issue, or, if it involves
another person and you'd rather not do that publicly, contact the
maintainer directly through GitHub.
