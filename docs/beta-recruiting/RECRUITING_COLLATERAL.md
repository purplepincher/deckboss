## 1. Forum/Facebook recruiting post (~150 words)

**Free fishing log. No account. Works with zero bars. Try it for 6–8 weeks.**

We're running a field beta of DeckBoss, a voice-first fishing logbook that runs in your browser. No app to install. No account to create. No DeckBoss server ever sees your data — it syncs straight to your own Google Drive or a zip you download yourself.

Here's what it does: tap a button, say what you caught, when, and where. That's it. Timestamp, GPS, and transcript happen automatically. It works offline. When you get signal back, it syncs.

This is a beta on 3–5 working boats for 6–8 weeks. It is not a replacement for your current log. It will not do your catch reporting for you. It is not evidence-grade and has not been reviewed as such.

We are not offering hardware, payment, or a support SLA. If something breaks, we fix it when we can. That's the deal.

If you want to see it working before you commit, video walkthrough is available. Reply here or DM.

## 4. Tester-facing "what we'll ask of you" sheet

### What we're asking from you in this beta

This is a 6–8 week field test of DeckBoss on 3–5 working boats. Here is exactly what participation involves. Read this before you commit. We will not surprise you with asks you didn't sign up for.

**Week one — run two logs.**
Keep your current logging method going alongside DeckBoss. Do not replace it yet. We need this parallel run to see how DeckBoss performs against what you're already using.

**Log at least once daily.**
Use DeckBoss for at least one recording per day, in genuinely loud or wet conditions. That's the point of an on-the-water test. We want to know how it holds up when it's loud, wet, or both.

**Expect a short daily check-in.**
Once per day, we will reach out by text or call to ask how the session went. This is a two-way conversation — we want to hear what's working and what isn't. It should take five minutes or less.

**That's it. No hardware, no payment, no support SLA.**

Here is what this beta does not include:

- **No hardware will be provided.** Use your own phone.
- **No compensation will be paid.** This is voluntary.
- **No support SLA is in effect.** If something breaks, we will fix it when we can, but we are not promising response times. This is a working beta, not a supported product.
- **No catch reporting integration.** DeckBoss is a personal log. It does not replace your existing catch reporting obligations and is not reviewed or certified as evidence-grade for legal or insurance purposes.
- **Your data is yours.** It never passes through a DeckBoss server. It syncs to your own storage. You can export a zip and keep it yourself at any time.

If any of this sounds like more than you want to commit to, that is fine — we are looking for the right fit, not maximum signups.
