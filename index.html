DeckBoss

Voice-first fishing log. No signal needed. Free.

Tap to record. Timestamp, GPS, and transcript happen automatically. Works offline. No account.

How it works

1. Tap the record button.

2. Talk. Say what you caught, where, how. Transcript happens automatically.

3. Done. Timestamp and GPS save automatically. Your entry is saved even with zero signal.

Built for a working deck

Offline by design. Everything works without cell signal. No account needed. Web Speech API runs offline after first load.

Tap only. No gestures. Built for gloves and wet hands.

Nothing is ever deleted. Entries are corrected via retract/amend events. Retracted entries stay recoverable via a Show Retracted toggle.

Your data, not ours

  • No account.
  • No DeckBoss server ever holds your data.
  • Syncs to your Google Drive, Cloudflare R2, Oracle Object Storage, or a plain .zip export.
  • Open source. MIT license.
